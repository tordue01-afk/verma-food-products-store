About Verma

Your reliable source for selected food products.

Verma Logistics Services is a food products business managed by Regina Mbaveren Ande in Adamawa State, Nigeria.

We supply melon seed, ogbono, groundnut oil, red palm oil, honey, dried pepper and other selected food products.

We serve households, retailers, restaurants, caterers, food vendors and other businesses.
